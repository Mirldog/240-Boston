Jobs of Boston

Competition is fierce in Boston, but because of this intense competition, the rewards are second to none. The business district of Boston is home to tons of companies, all ranging from small, startup type companies, all the way up to corporate giants. While Boston is an expensive city, the salaries make up for it. The average person in any industry at any level will make around $71,000 in salary. Lots of larger companies have been moving to Boston recently as well, so it is always a good place to get your foot in the door.

Amazon

Just this past February, Amazon Services have moved their headquarters to Boston, thus bringing in 2,000 highly competitive and high paying jobs. Amazon made the move to Boston thanks to its great schooling systems, high brain power, and booming technological economy. More and more tech companies have made the move to Boston, and it has shown to pay dividends, as companies continue to thrive on the Harbor.

Liberty Mutual

You wouldn't expect to see liberty mutual on this list, considering their logo is a picture of the Statue of Liberty. I certainly would have expected their HQ to be in NY. However their headquarters are in Boston. Libery Mutual is an insurance agency. It is a fortune 500 company that employs over 50,000 people.

Boston Consulting Group

The Boston Consulting Group is a multinational consulting firm. Consistently, BCG is ranked in the top ten of "The Best Companies to Work at" by Forbes Magazine. Last year, the Boston Giant ranked fourth. This is thanks to its positive work environment, and excellent employee benefits. You need to be at the top of your game to land this job however, as Glassdoor.com ranks it the 3rd most difficult company with which to interview.
