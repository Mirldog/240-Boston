Boston's Professional Sports

Boston has a long history of sports, with different teams showing dominance in different eras, but over the past decade, Boston sports have shown dominance across the board. It is an exciting time to be a Boston sports fan. The four major teams in Boston are The Boston Red Sox for baseball, The Boston Celtics for Basketball, The Boston Bruins for Hockey, and The New England Patriots for Football.
